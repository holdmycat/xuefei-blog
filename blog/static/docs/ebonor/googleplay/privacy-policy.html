1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

1.1 Account Registration and Login

When you create or log in to a game account, we collect your phone number and password to create your account, verify your identity, and protect account security. Without this information we cannot provide account-based services.

1.2 Game Services

To operate the Game and deliver its features, we collect:

  • Device identifier (Android ID) – for account security and service stability.
  • Device information (device name, model, OS version) – for compatibility and performance optimization.
  • IP address – for network communication, security controls, and routing to the nearest server.
  • Sensor data (accelerometer, gyroscope) – used solely by the Unity engine for rendering and performance adaptation; not used to infer your physical location.
  • Game data (save files, battle records, character progress) – to store and restore your in-game progress.

1.3 Real-Name Verification (Mainland China Users Only)

Pursuant to Chinese law, users in mainland China must complete real-name verification. We collect your full name, ID document type, and ID document number solely for identity verification and minor-protection compliance.

1.4 Security and Anti-Cheat

To maintain a fair gameplay environment, we collect device identifiers, IP address, and access logs (date and time) to detect cheating software, bots, and other violations of our Terms of Service.

1.5 Device Permissions

We request permissions only when you trigger the relevant feature. We do not request permissions at first launch.

Permission When Requested Purpose Can Be Denied?
Storage (READ / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) Saving game data, screenshots, or downloading game resources Reading and writing game resources and save files Yes (some features will be limited)
Internet (INTERNET) Game launch Online gameplay and hot-update delivery No (required for online play)

You can revoke granted permissions at any time in Settings → Apps → Ebonor → Permissions on your device.

2. How We Store Your Information

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law. When the retention period expires, we will delete or anonymize your data.

Our servers are located in mainland China. If cross-border data transfer becomes necessary, we will comply with applicable laws, obtain your consent where required, and ensure equivalent protection at the destination.

4. Sharing, Transfer, and Disclosure

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., cloud infrastructure, analytics) who process data on our behalf and under our instructions, bound by confidentiality obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only when:

  • You have given explicit consent;

Transfer

We will not transfer your personal information to any third party. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, we will notify you and require the new data controller to honor this Policy or obtain fresh consent.

Public Disclosure

We do not publicly disclose your personal information except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. How We Protect Your Information

  • We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures—including network isolation, data encryption, and access controls—to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
  • No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
  • In the event of a data security incident, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law, and take prompt remedial action.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Correction

You may review and correct your personal information through Settings → Account in the Game, or by contacting us directly.

Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information if we have collected or used it unlawfully, or when you close your account or we cease operations.

Withdraw Consent

You may withdraw your consent to data processing by revoking device permissions (see Section 1.5). Withdrawal does not affect processing already carried out before the withdrawal.

Account Deletion

To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account in the Game, or contact us. Account deletion is irreversible; we will stop collecting your information and delete or anonymize your account data.

Response Time

We will respond to verified requests within 15 business days at no charge for reasonable requests.

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for users aged 13 and older (per the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, COPPA) and 14 and older for users in mainland China.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below these age thresholds. If a parent or guardian believes we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

Users under 18 in mainland China are subject to anti-addiction restrictions as required by Chinese law.
