Account Deletion Request

Ebonor: Dark Survivor

Package: com.zxf.ebonor

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. Once your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Game save data and progress
  • Device identifiers linked to your account
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Note: Account deletion is irreversible. Your game progress cannot be recovered after deletion.

Option 1: Delete from within the Game

  1. Open Ebonor: Dark Survivor
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your request

Your account and data will be deleted within 30 days of confirmation.

Option 2: Submit a Request by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: clashancients@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request – Ebonor

Please include: The phone number or account ID associated with your account

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 15 business days.

Data Retention

Upon account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted or anonymized within 30 days
  • Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days before being purged from all systems
  • Data required to be retained by law (e.g., transaction records) will be kept for the legally required period only
